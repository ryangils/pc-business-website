Our Story

CyberForge was founded in 2019 by Ryan, a lifelong PC enthusiast who grew tired of the overpriced, under-spec'd machines being sold to everyday consumers. Armed with a soldering iron, a passion for performance, and a garage full of components — CyberForge was born.

What started as a side project has grown into a full-service custom PC shop trusted by over 500 customers across the UK. We build, repair, and upgrade machines for everyone from first-time buyers to professional streamers and game developers.

Every build is stress-tested, cable-managed to perfection, and backed by our industry-leading 3-year warranty. We don't ship mediocrity — we ship machines we'd be proud to run ourselves.
