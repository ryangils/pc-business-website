The Classic Recipe

Our legendary home-style garlic bread. No chef skills required — just butter and bravery.

🧄 Ingredients

  • 1 large baguette or ciabatta loaf
  • 115g (½ cup) unsalted butter, softened
  • 6–8 cloves of fresh garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp sea salt flakes
  • ¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Pinch of dried chilli flakes (optional)
  • 50g Parmesan, freshly grated (optional)

🔥 Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 200 °C (180 °C fan) / 400 °F.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper together until fully combined.
  3. Slice the baguette diagonally at 2 cm intervals — don't cut all the way through.
  4. Generously spread the garlic butter into each cut, getting right into every crevice.
  5. Wrap tightly in foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Open the foil and bake a further 5 minutes until golden and gloriously crisp.
  7. Scatter Parmesan over the top, return to oven for 2 minutes until melted.
  8. Serve immediately. Try not to eat the whole loaf in one sitting. (You will fail.)
